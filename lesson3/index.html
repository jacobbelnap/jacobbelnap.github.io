Mr. Snowday Site Plan

A site plan for MrSnowDay.com, the snowman that talks!

By Jacob Belnap

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to display the weather and snowdays of every school district in the United States. Visitors will be able to see in real time what schools have snowdays and the predictions for snow days in the upcoming 10 days. Stats will also be displayed for last snowday and # of snowdays in the current year.
