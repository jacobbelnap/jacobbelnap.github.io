Site Audience

This site is for students, parents, teachers, and anyone interested in knowing what schools are canceled in their area for the day.

Persona 1

Bart is a student from Illinois and loves snowdays. Every year Bart's school usually cancels school due to snow. He always struggles to find out whether or not his school is cancelled. This site will give him easy access to the information he seeks.

Persona 2

Seymour is a principal at a local elementary school. Every winter he gets loaded with phone calls from teachers, students, and parents asking whether the weather permits a snowday. This site should limit those phone calls because everyone will be informed.
